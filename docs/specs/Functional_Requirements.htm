List of Requirements

Name

R1 - Add a new book to the catalog.

Summary

We want to add a new book to the catalog so as to put it on sale.

Inputs

Title of the book.

ISBN of the book.

Price of the book.

Results

The catalog is updated with the addition of the new book.

Name

R2 - Add a book to the client's shopping cart.

Summary

A client can add items that hes interested in to the shopping cart. Each time he adds an item, he must enter a book and the amount of copies that he wants.

Inputs

Book that the client is going to buy.

Amount of copies that he's looking to buy.

Results

The shopping cart has added the new purchase item.

The total for that purchase item is calculated.

The total for the whole cart is calculated.

Name

R3 - remove an item from the shopping cart.

Summary

The client wishes to eliminate all the copies of one book from the shopping cart.

Inputs

ISBN for the book that is to be deleted

Results

The shopping cart is updated without the deleted item.
